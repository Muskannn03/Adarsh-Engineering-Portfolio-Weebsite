Who We Are

Specialists in Advanced Architectural Façades

Adarsh Engineering Fabricators specializes in sourcing, supplying, and expertly installing high-quality façade materials tailored to our clients' precise needs. Our team brings deep structural engineering and process designs to every project, ensuring execution accuracy and timely delivery.

Our extensive expertise ranges from aluminum composite panels (ACP), sun breakers, structural glazing, and curtain walls to aluminum fins, louvers, sliding windows, and custom metal fabrication solutions.

Manufacturing Powerhouse

In-House Fabrication Workshop

Active Production Facility

Engineered for Precision & High-Volume Output

All structural canopy framing, facade brackets, supporting grids, and customized metalwork are pre-fabricated in our fully equipped, modern workshop. By fabricating components in a controlled environment, we ensure 100% precision, eliminate environmental delays, and guarantee superior joint strength.

Heavy Shearing & Bending

Industrial machinery capable of cutting and folding heavy-gauge steel plates to exact design specifications.

Certified Welding Operations

High-grade structural welding (including Shielded Metal Arc and CO2 welding) for load-bearing joints.

Surface Preparation & Coating

Dedicated finishing area for shot-blasting, anti-corrosive primer painting, and protective coatings to withstand severe weather.

Trial Assembly & Quality Checks

Pre-assembly testing of complex structures in the shop to ensure rapid, zero-error installation once delivered to site.

SAFETY FIRST
Zero Compromise

Our Goal: Zero Loss Time Accident

"It is Adarsh Engineering Fabricators' Policy to provide and implement a safe and healthy work environment for conducting its business, and thereby achieve zero loss time accidents and prevent damage to property or environment."

1. Induction Training

Every worker undergoes a detailed safety induction process prior to site deployment.

2. Daily Tool Box Talks

Daily briefing covering work permit systems, working at heights, electrical safety, and hot works.

3. Monthly Mass Training & Safety Awards

Monthly training sessions and motivational rewards given to top safety performers.

Mandatory Site PPE (Personal Protective Equipment)

Hard Hats

Compulsory for all workers on entering the site

Safety Belts

Mandatory for height jobs > 2 meters

Safety Shoes

Steel-toe safety shoes for foot protection

Goggles & Masks

Eye & respiratory protection on active jobs

Structural Scaffolding Standards

We mandate and utilize **strictly MS scaffolding systems** across all project locations to eliminate structural failures.
